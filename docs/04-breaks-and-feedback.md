# Phase 4: Breaks & Feedback

## How Zen Mode Works

When a work session ends, the app opens **Zen Mode** â€“ a full-screen break page in your browser.

- **Short Break (5 min):** Opens `break.html` with a countdown timer.
- **Long Break (15 min):** Same page, longer duration.
- **Lunch (60 min):** Extended break with the same calming interface.

### Why a Browser Page?

This is an intentional "focus hijack." By taking over your screen, you're forced to step away from work. The page includes:

- A countdown timer
- Random entertainment links (optional)
- Calming animations

> **Tip:** Don't close the page immediately â€“ use the time to stretch, hydrate, or look away from the screen!
